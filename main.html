Paris


Paris, often called the City of Light, is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world. It is famous for its historic landmarks, rich culture, art, fashion, and delicious cuisine.

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – a world-famous avenue.
  5. Seine River Cruise – a beautiful way to explore the city.
  • Croissants – buttery and flaky French pastries.
  • Macarons – colorful and sweet treats.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with delicious fillings.
  • French Cheese – rich and flavorful varieties.
  • Baguette – classic French bread.
