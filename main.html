Paris


Paris, often called the City of Light, is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, romantic atmosphere, and world-class art and cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the famous Mona Lisa
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture
  4. Champs-Élysées – a vibrant and famous avenue
  5. Sacré-Cœur – offers breathtaking city views

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – buttery and flaky breakfast pastries
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings
  • Macarons – colorful and delicate French desserts
  • Baguette – a classic French bread
  • Cheese varieties – rich and diverse flavors
