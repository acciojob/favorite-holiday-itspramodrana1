Paris


Paris, often called the City of Light, is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world. It is famous for its historic landmarks, art, fashion, and rich culture.

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a Gothic architectural wonder.
  4. Champs-Élysées – a world-famous avenue.
  5. Seine River Cruise – a scenic experience.
  • Croissants – buttery French pastries.
  • Macarons – colorful sweet treats.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with fillings.
  • French Cheese – rich and flavorful varieties.
  • Baguette – classic French bread.
